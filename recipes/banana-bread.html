Banana Bread

Description

This is an excellent homemade Banana Bread. Minimum ingredients are required.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients
  2. Bake to perfection
  3. Let stand for 5 minutes
